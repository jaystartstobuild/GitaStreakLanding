Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Your request will be processed within 30 days.

What will be permanently deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All lesson progress and streak history
  • Your Karma Coins balance
  • Any linked account data (Apple ID or Google account association)

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to request deletion

Account deletion is handled via email to keep the process secure and verifiable. Follow these four steps:

1

Send an email to our support team

Compose a new email addressed to support@jeystack.com. You can use the button below to open a pre-filled draft instantly.

2

Use the subject line: Delete My Account Request

This subject line helps our team route your request to the right place quickly so it gets processed without delays.

3

Include your account details

In the body of your email, include the email address associated with your Gita Streak account and your full name. This helps us verify your identity before processing the request.

4

We'll take it from here

Our team will verify your identity, permanently delete your account and all associated data, and send you a confirmation email. The entire process is completed within 30 days of receiving your request.

California Residents — CCPA

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to request the deletion of personal information we have collected about you. This page fulfills that right. We will process your verified deletion request within 45 days as required by California law. If we need additional time, we may extend this period by up to an additional 45 days and will notify you of the extension.

Other US State Residents

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, Montana, Iowa, Indiana, and Tennessee also have the right to request deletion of personal data under their respective state privacy laws (VCDPA, CPA, CTDPA, UCPA, TDPSA, OCPA, MCDPA, ICDPA, INCDPA, and TIPA). We honor these rights equally. Use the email process above to submit your request, and we will process it in accordance with the applicable law in your state.

Streaks reset automatically if a daily lesson is missed. If you believe your streak was lost due to a technical issue, please email us at support@jeystack.com with the email address associated with your account. We'll look into it and do our best to make it right.

Karma Coins are Gita Streak's in-app virtual currency. You earn them by completing daily lessons, maintaining streaks, and hitting milestones. They can be used to unlock bonus content and cosmetic features within the app. Karma Coins have no real-world monetary value and cannot be exchanged for cash or redeemed outside of Gita Streak.

# 🕉️ Gita Streak

> **Ancient wisdom. Modern habits.**
> Master the Bhagavad Gita in just 1 to 5 minutes a day. 100% Free. No Ads. No Paywalls. Ever.

Gita Streak is a mobile application (built with React Native for iOS and Android) designed to help users establish a daily habit (Sadhana) of studying the Bhagavad Gita. This repository contains the source code for the **Gita Streak Landing Page** (accessible at [gitastreak.com](https://gitastreak.com/)).

---

## 🌟 Core App Features

The mobile app is built around a daily spiritual practice (Sadhana) divided into four primary activities, requiring only 5 minutes a day:

### 1. The Daily Sadhana Quartet
*   **📖 Read (Verse by Verse):** Explore all 701 sacred verses of the Bhagavad Gita, broken down into 120 bite-sized, plain-English lessons designed to fit into a single minute.
*   **🌬️ Breathe (Find Your Calm):** Perform guided breathing exercises to cultivate mindfulness, with **Gopi** (your friendly guide) by your side.
*   **✍️ Journal (Reflect Daily):** Write down your personal reflections on each verse to track your spiritual and personal growth over time.
*   **🧠 Quiz (Test Yourself):** Take a quick daily 5-question quiz to test your comprehension, earn Karma Coins, and level up.

### 2. Gamified Habit Building
*   **🔥 Daily Streaks:** Stay consistent in your practice and build a long-term habit of daily study.
*   **🪙 Karma Coins & Ranks:** Earn Karma Coins by completing quizzes and maintaining streaks. Move through **22 distinct ranks** of spiritual progression, climbing towards liberation.
*   **➕ Custom Habits:** Blend your Gita study with custom personal routines (e.g., "morning meditation" or "evening journaling") to build a complete daily lifestyle routine.

### 3. Smart & Personalized Experience
*   **🔔 Intelligent Reminders:** Schedule customizable push notifications (e.g., daily at 7:00 AM) to prompt your practice at the perfect time.
*   **🐮 Gopi your Guide:** Interact with Gopi, a friendly cow guide, who guides you through breathing, study, and reflection.

## 💻 Landing Page Architecture

The landing page located in this repository is designed to be highly responsive, interactive, and visually stunning.

### Features:
*   **Interactive Design:** Features fluid animations (flame pulse, Gopi float, progress bars), smooth scroll behaviors, and responsive layouts.
*   **Custom Particle Canvas & Effects:** Includes ambient gradient glows that react to the cursor or viewport scroll.
*   **Tweakable Moods/Motions:** A system built to dynamically update themes based on state triggers (`data-mood` support: reverent, modern, joyful).
*   **SEO Optimized:** Semantic HTML5, structured meta tags for Twitter/OpenGraph, and optimized loading sequences.
*   **Fully Responsive:** Tailored layouts for both mobile devices (iPhone SE/short phones to modern viewports) and desktops.

2. Description of Service

Gita Streak is a mobile application available on iOS and Android that helps users develop a daily habit of engaging with the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. The Service includes:

  • Bhagavad Gita Lessons. A structured library of bite-sized, approachable lessons drawn from the 18 chapters and 700 verses of the Bhagavad Gita, designed for modern learners.
  • Streak Tracking. A daily streak system that encourages consistent engagement by tracking consecutive days of lesson completion and rewarding habit formation.
  • Karma Coins. A virtual in-app reward currency earned through completing lessons, maintaining streaks, and achieving milestones. Karma Coins may be used to unlock certain in-app features or content as described within the app.
  • Gopi Guide Character. An animated in-app guide character named Gopi who accompanies users through lessons, provides encouragement, and delivers contextual commentary to enrich the learning experience.

The Maker

Jeyanth Kothari

Founder · Engineer

Hi, I'm Jeyanth.

I grew up with the Gita on the bookshelf, admired but never really read. When I finally sat down with it as an adult, it changed how I think about work, stress, and who I'm trying to become.

I wanted to build something that could do the same for anyone, anywhere. Completely free, no distractions. Just the teaching.

The wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita deserves to be in everyone's hands. Not locked behind paywalls, not buried under ads, not watered down.

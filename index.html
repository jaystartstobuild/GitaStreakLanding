The Maker

Hi, I'm Jeyanth.

I grew up with the Gita on the bookshelf admired but never really read. When I finally sat down with it as an adult, it changed how I think about work, stress and who I'm trying to become.

The Gita changed how I think about work, pressure, and purpose. I wanted to build something that could do the same for anyone, anywhere. Completely free, no distractions, just the teaching.

I built Gita Streak because the wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita deserves to be in everyone's hands. Not locked behind paywalls, not buried under ads, not watered down.
